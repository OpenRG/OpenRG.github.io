About Us


OpenRG experts provide support packages and modeling services to business and government. As a public benefit corporation, our mission is to promote the growth of open modeling and to benefit the open-source, open data, and open science ecosystem.

OpenRG creates a strong incentive for connecting modeling teams with profitable opportunities; facilitates incentive-based compensation for OpenRG employees that rewards them based on the success of OpenRG experts and the firm; and side steps competition with open-source foundations, think tanks, and academic institutions for tax-deductible charitable contributions.
