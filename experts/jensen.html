Matthew Jensen

Matthew Jensen is a co-founder of the Open Research Group and a Principal for information security and tax policy.

Jensen is co-creator of several open-source projects including IRS Disclosures, Tax-Calculator, Policy Simulation Library, and Compute Studio. He was the founding director of the at the American Enterprise Institute from 2012-2022.

His work and writing have been featured in the Wall Street Journal, the New York Times, the Washington Post, CNN, Fox News, MSNBC, and Tax Notes among other news and professional publications.

Jensen is a graduate of Pomona College with a degree in math. He grew up in Indiana and lives in New Jersey with his wife and their two children.
